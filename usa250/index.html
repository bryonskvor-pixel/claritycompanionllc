The 250-Year Macro Audit

America at 250 is an experiment defined by extreme divergence. It is simultaneously the wealthiest, most technologically dominant superpower in human history, and an intensely polarized state wrestling with crumbling social trust and systemic inequalities.

The Great Changes

The shift from 3 million citizens on an agrarian coastline to a multi-ethnic transcontinental giant of 340+ million wired by advanced AI systems.

The Big Successes

Unmatched institutional longevity under a single constitution, driving global scientific breakthroughs, industrial revolutions, and space commerce ventures.

The Core Failures

Systemic sorting of civic outcomes by wealth/ZIP code, high rates of preventable mortality, and acute internal political fracturing.
